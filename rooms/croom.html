The Children's Room!

Welcome to the children's room, where Gumball and Darwins Adventures start! This space captures the essence of the mischievous duo, filled with bold colors, playful decor, and a touch of chaos that only Gumball and Darwin could bring. The room features bunk beds for an authentic experience, decorated with posters, quirky toys, and little nods to their endless adventures. Whether you’re a fan of Gumball’s outlandish ideas or Darwin’s warm-hearted charm, this room invites you to dive into the animated fun and feel like part of the Watterson family!

  • Room Prices: Starting at $150 per night. Special rates available for weekly bookings and off-season stays.

  • Room Amenities:
    • Bunk beds with cozy themed bedding
    • Interactive decor inspired by Gumball and Darwin
    • Complimentary Wi-Fi
    • Flat-screen TV with streaming services
    • Plush toys and fun games for kids
    • Nightlight for a comforting sleep
